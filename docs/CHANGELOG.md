# Changelog

## [0.1.1] - 2026-07-14

### Changed
- Cooldown reduced from 5 seconds to 2 seconds
- The cannon now fires a projectile that travels to your target instead of exploding instantly
- On impact, the projectile detonates a massive cluster of explosions for huge world damage

## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-13

### Added
- Orbital Cannon item — a fishing rod-styled weapon that calls down explosive strikes
- Right-click to fire an explosion at whatever you're looking at (up to 100 blocks away)
- 5-second cooldown between shots
- Available in the Combat creative tab
